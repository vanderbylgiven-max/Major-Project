College Overview

Limkokwing University of Creative Technology is a pioneering private institution founded in 1991, headquartered in Cyberjaya, Malaysia, with campuses across Asia, Africa, and Europe. With over 30,000 students from 150+ countries, the university is a melting pot of cultures, ideas, and innovation.
