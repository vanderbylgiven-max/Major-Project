Application Procedure

Follow these simple steps to complete your application.

1

Check Requirements

Review the admission requirements for your chosen programme to ensure you qualify.

2

Complete Application

Fill out the online application form below or download the PDF form and submit in person.

3

Submit Documents

Submit certified copies of your certificates, ID, and passport photos to the admissions office.

4

Await Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email or letter within 5–10 working days of your application.

5

Pay & Register

Upon acceptance, complete payment of the registration fee and collect your student ID.

Admission Requirements

Minimum requirements vary by programme level. Check both general and specific requirements below.

General Requirements

  • Namibian Senior Secondary Certificate (NSSC) or equivalent
  • Minimum of 22 and 25 points on the IGCSE scale
  • Certified copy of National Identity Document or Passport
  • Certified copy of academic results
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs

IT Programme Requirements

  • All general requirements apply
  • Minimum grade C in Mathematics
  • Pass in Physical Science (recommended)
  • For Diploma:22 points in 5 Subjects Grade 11/12 or completion of relevant Certificate level
  • For Degree: 25 points in 5 Subjects Grade 11/12 or completion of relevant Diploma in IT or related field with at least a credit pass
  • For Mature Age entry applicants: Grade 10 plus three (3) years of work experience letter

International Students

  • Equivalent qualifications from home country
  • Valid passport (minimum 1 year validity)
  • Student visa or study permit
  • Proof of medical insurance
  • Translated and certified academic transcripts
  • Proof of financial support / sponsorship letter

Mature Entry (25+)

  • Minimum age of 25 at time of application
  • Relevant work experience of at least 3 years in the field
  • Employer reference letter
  • Portfolio or work samples (where applicable)
  • Written motivation letter (minimum 500 words)
  • Interview may be required
