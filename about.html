About Limkokwing University

The Global University of Creative Technology

Our Identity

Limkokwing University is a multi-campus, global university with a presence across three continents. Our Namibia campus serves as a hub for creativity and innovation, empowering students with the skills needed for the 21st-century digital economy.

Our Vision

To be the leading global university for creative technology, fostering innovation and industry-ready graduates.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education that merges industry requirements with creative expression.

Why Choose LUCT Namibia?

  • Industry Integration: Learning through real-world projects.
  • Global Networking: Access to a worldwide community of creators.
  • Creative Environment: State-of-the-art facilities designed for inspiration.
